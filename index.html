About Me

Hello! Thanks for visiting my site.

I'm Chantel, and I am a soon-to-be new graduate in Computer Science who loves to put a creative spin on things and learn at every opportunity.

Some of my accomplishments include Associate of the Quarter and Associate of the Month at Sysco Food Services, and a Certificate of Excellence in General Sciences at Highline College. I am currently studying Computer Science and Systems at University of Washington Tacoma, graduating in Spring 2021.

I am passionate about new experiences, whether it be a new project, traveling to a new place, trying a new food, or coding in a new language. I believe that it is new experiences that enrich life, inspire new ideas, and foster personal growth.

I am also passionate about about arts in a variety of mediums and expressions including watercolor painting, oil painting, digital art, and film photography. I also enjoy spending time surrounded by nature such as hiking, walking my dog, or visiting Seattle parks, National Parks, or State Parks that we are lucky enough to have access to here in the Pacific Northwest. I'm always eager for a new challenge or adventure!
